# Round 01 Feedback Example

Shot: s1-2

Observed problem:
- The new clip may start from a wider camera scale than the previous shot.
- The protagonist should not stand up while the emergency broadcast is still speaking.
- The warning beeps should remain audible after the broadcast, then become quieter under the boss dialogue.

Optimization direction:
- Lock the first frame to the previous shot's final framing and office layout.
- Keep the protagonist seated during the entire broadcast.
- Let the boss speak only after the broadcast has fully ended.
- Keep the red alarm beeps as background sound while the boss speaks.

Optimization direction:
