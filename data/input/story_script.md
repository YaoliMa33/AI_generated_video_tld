# The Last Data

A future architectural data worker believes that scanning and uploading civilization data is meaningless routine work. In a cold data office, the protagonist is pressured by a boss to keep uploading architectural scan data even as an extinction-level impact alert interrupts the workday.

The conflict escalates because the boss still demands that the data upload be completed before evacuation. The protagonist finally breaks down, asking what the work is for if the world is ending.

After the catastrophe, the protagonist wakes in the ruins of the city. The office, the buildings, and the old civilization appear destroyed. In the ruins, the protagonist discovers a hard drive marked `CIVILIZATION ARCHIVE`.

When the hard drive activates, the stored architectural data becomes the basis for rebuilding the city. Blue holographic scans and architectural wireframes rise from the ruins, suggesting that the data once considered meaningless has become the last surviving blueprint of civilization.
